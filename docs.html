Separation Logic

ACM/IEEE Symposium on Logic in Computer Science (LICS), 2002.

Automated Verification of Shape, Size and Bag Properties via User-Defined Predicates in Separation Logic, by Wei-Ngan Chin, Cristina David, Huu Hai Nguyen, Shengchao Qin.
Journal of Science of Computer Programming, 2012.
